Functional Threshold Power (FTP)

Tushen Horo na Tushen Iko

Abubuwan Da Suka Kamata A Sani

  • Mene ne: FTP shine mafi girman matsakaicin ikon da zaku iya dorewa na kimanin sa'a 1 ba tare da gajiya ba
  • Yadda ake Gwaji: Mafi yawan amfani shine jagoran gwajin minti 20: 95% na mafi kyawun matsakaicin ikon minti 20 dinku
  • Me ya sa yake da Mahimmanci: FTP yana ba da damar keɓaɓɓun yankunan iko, lissafin TSS daidai, da bin diddigin lafiyar jiki ta hanyar bayanai
  • Ƙimar da ake sawa: Amatir: 2.0-3.0 W/kg | Mai gasa: 3.5-4.5 W/kg | Ƙwararru: 5.5-6.5 W/kg
  • Sau nawa ake gwaji: Sake gwadawa kowane mako 6-8 a lokacin bukukuwan horo don sabunta yankunan yayin da lafiyar jiki ta inganta

Mene ne FTP?

Functional Threshold Power (FTP) shine mafi girman matsakaicin ikon da zaku iya dorewa na kimanin sa'a guda ba tare da tara gajiya ba. Yana wakiltar kofar aerobic dinku—iyaka tsakanin ƙoƙari mai dorewa da wanda ba zai dore ba. FTP yana zama tushen dukkan horo na tushen wutar lantarki, yana ba da damar yankunan horo na keɓaɓɓu da ƙididdigar nauyin horo daidai.

Functional Threshold Power ya juya horo na hawan keke a farkon shekarun 2000 ta hanyar ba da ma'auni ɗaya mai amfani da ke bayyana iyakar ilimin jikin ku. Ba kamar gwajin lactate na dakin gwaje-gwaje ba, ana iya auna FTP da ma'aunin iko kawai da hanya ta buɗe.

🎯 Mahimmancin Ilimin Jiki

FTP yana daidaita sosai da:

  • Kofar Lactate 2 (LT2) - Kofar numfashi ta biyu
  • Matsakaicin Madawwamiyar Lactate (MLSS) - Kimanin 88.5% na ainihin FTP
  • Critical Power (CP) - Yawanci cikin ±5W na FTP
  • ~4 mmol/L lactate na jini - Alamar OBLA ta al'ada

Me ya sa FTP Yake da Mahimmanci

Functional Threshold Power shine ma'aunin tushe wanda ke buɗe dukkan horo na tushen iko masu zurfi:

  • Yankunan Horo na Iko: Yana keɓance yankunan zafi dangane da ilimin jikin ku
  • Lissafin TSS: Yana ba da damar ƙididdigar Skor Damuwar Horo daidai
  • CTL/ATL/TSB: Ana buƙatarsa don ma'aunai na Performance Management Chart
  • Bin Diddigin Ci Gaba: Ma'auni na zahiri na ingantuwar ikon iyaka akan lokaci
  • Tsarin Tsere: Yana ƙayyade ikon da ake iya dorewa don gwajin lokaci da tsare na hanya
⚠️ Dogaro Mai Mahimmanci: Ba tare da gwajin FTP daidai ba, ba za a iya lissafin ma'aunai masu zurfi na nauyin horo (TSS, Intensity Factor, CTL, ATL, TSB) yadda ya kamata ba. FTP mara daidaituwa zai lalata dukkan binciken horo na tushen iko da ya biyo baya.

Me ya sa Ake Zaɓar FTP?

FTP yana samun daidaituwa ta cikakke tsakanin daidaituwa da amfani. Ba kamar Critical Power (wanda ke buƙatar gwaje-gwaje da yawa) ko gwajin lactate na dakin gwaje-gwaje (mai tsada da rashin dacewa), ana iya auna FTP a cikin ƙoƙari guda na minti 20. Wannan yana sa ya yi kyau don sake gwadawa akai-akai kowane mako 6-8 don bin diddigin ci gaban lafiya da sabunta yankunan horo.

Yadda Ake Gwada FTP Naku

Ƙa'idoji uku da aka tabbatar don ƙayyade Functional Threshold Power naku

🏆 Gwajin FTP na Minti 20

Hanyar da Ake Amfani da ita Fiye

  1. Dumi-dumi (minti 20)

    Juyi mai sauƙi, ƙara zafi a hankali. Haɗa fasaha 2-3 na gajere a gudu na tsere.

  2. Minti 5 na Dukkan Ƙoƙari

    Ƙoƙari mai ɗorewar mafi girma don ƙaƙaƙe rezervolin anaerobic. Kada ku tsaya baya.

  3. Hutawa (minti 10)

    Juyi mai sauƙi don share lactate. Bari bugun zuciya ya faɗi ƙasa da 120 bpm.

  4. Gwajin Minti 20

    Ƙoƙari mai ɗorewar dukkan ikon. Rike iko daidai—kada ku ƙare da sauri. Rubuta matsakaicin ikon.

  5. Lissafin FTP

    FTP = 95% na matsakaicin ikon minti 20

    Misali: 250W na minti 20 → FTP = 238W

💡 Shawarar Ƙwararru: Nufin rabe-raben iko daidai. Idan ba za ku iya rike iko a minti 5 na ƙarshe ba, kun fara da ƙarfi. Tsarin ya fi mahimmanci don samun sakamakon daidai.

⚡ Gwajin Ramp

Madadin da Ya fi Gajarta (Minti 20-30 lapapọ̀)

  1. Dumi-dumi (minti 10)

    Juyi mai sauƙi a gudu na jimiri don shirya ƙafafu.

  2. Tsarin Ramp

    Fara da ikon mai sauƙi (100-150W). Ƙara 20W kowane minti har sai gajiyawa.

  3. Hawa Har zuwa Gazawa

    Ci gaba har sai ba za ku iya rike ikon da ake nufin ba. Rubuta mafi girman matsakaicin ikon minti 1.

  4. Lissafin FTP

    FTP = 75% na mafi girman ikon minti 1

    Misali: Mafi girma minti 1 na 340W → FTP = 255W

✅ Fa'idodi: Ba ya ɗaukar hankali sosai, yana da gajeren tsawon lokaci, mai sauƙi. Ana son shi a dandamali na horo na cikin gida kamar Zwift da TrainerRoad.

🥇 Gwajin Minti 60

Ma'aunin Gaskiya (Mafi Daidaituwa)

  1. Dumi-dumi (minti 20)

    Dumi-dumi mai ci gaba tare da ƙoƙarori da yawa na gudu na tsere don shirya aiki mai tsauri na iyaka.

  2. Ƙoƙari Mai Girma na Minti 60

    Dukkan ƙoƙari mai ɗorewar sa'a guda. Tsarin shine komai—fara da hankali.

  3. Rubuta Matsakaicin Ikon

    Matsakaicin ikonku na cikakken minti 60 shine ainihin FTP naku. Babu lissafin da ake buƙata.

⚠️ Mafi Daidaituwa, Amma Mafi Wahala: Gwajin minti 60 yana da wahala sosai kuma yana gajiyar da hankali. Yawancin masu hawa suna amfani da tsarin minti 20 don sake gwadawa akai-akai kuma suna ajiye gwajin sa'a guda don tabbatarwa ko ƙididdiga aiki na kololuwa.

🔄 Yanayin Gwaji Ya Fi Mahimmanci

Don samun sakamako daidai da ake iya kwatantawa:

  • Cikin gida vs Waje: Gwaje-gwaje na cikin gida sun fi iko (babu iska, ababen hawa, ƙasa) amma suna iya zama 5-10W ƙasa saboda taruwar zafi
  • Lokacin Rana: Gwada a lokacin da kuke yawan horo don sakamakon da ake iya kwatantawa
  • Ruwa & Abinci: An cika ruwa da abinci sosai, amma ba bayan cin abinci nan da nan ba
  • Yanayin Gajiya: Gwada lokacin da kuke sabo, ba bayan bukukuwan horo masu wahala ba
  • Ma'aunin Iko: Yi amfani da ma'aunin iko ɗaya don dukkan gwaje-gwaje don guje wa bambancin daidaitawa

🔄 Yaushe Za a Sake Gwada FTP

Sabunta FTP naku kowane mako 6-8 a lokacin bukukuwan horo mai aiki, ko lokacin da:

  • Kuna iya kammala horo a kima sama da yankunan da aka tsara akai-akai
  • Bugun zuciyarku ya ragu a ikon da yake daidai
  • Bayan canjin mataki na horo (ginin tushe → ginin → kololuwa)
  • Bayan manyan canje-canje na lafiya (rauni, rashin lafiya, lokacin hutun kakar)
  • Kafin fara sabon shirin horo ko kakar tsere

FTP vs Critical Power: Mene ne Bambance?

Duka FTP da Critical Power (CP) suna bayyana iyakarku, amma suna amfani da hanyoyi daban-daban.

Functional Threshold Power (FTP)

  • Lamba ɗaya: Ƙimar iko ɗaya tana bayyana iyakarku
  • Sauƙin gwaji: Ƙoƙari guda na minti 20 ko 60
  • An karɓa sosai: Ma'aunin masana'antu tun farkon shekarun 2000
  • Sauƙin fahimta: "Mafi girman ikon sa'a 1"
  • Mai amfani: Mai sauri don sake gwadawa akai-akai
  • Iyaka: Ba ya lissafin ƙarfin anaerobic (W')

Critical Power (CP)

  • Tsarin ɓangarori biyu: CP (iko mai ɗorewar) + W' (ƙarfin anaerobic)
  • Mafi rikitarwa: Yana buƙatar ƙoƙarori 3-4 na mafi girma a tsawon lokaci daban-daban
  • Mafi daidaituwa: Yana samfura alaƙar iko-tsawon lokaci daidai
  • Yana ba da damar ma'aunin W': Yana bin diddigin ragewa/farfadowar "batirin anaerobic"
  • Mafi kyau don ƙoƙarori masu canjawa: Tsare na hanya, criteriums, MTB
  • Musayar: Ƙarin lokacin gwaji, bincike mafi rikitarwa

Wane ne Ya Kamata Ka Yi Amfani da shi?

  • Yi amfani da FTP idan: Kuna son yankunan horo masu sauƙi da amfani don ƙoƙarori mai daidaituwa (gwajin lokaci, triathlon, hawan tudu)
  • Yi amfani da CP idan: Kuna son samfurar daidai na ƙoƙarori masu canjawa-zafi (criteriums, tsare na hanya, MTB) kuma kuna buƙatar bin diddigin ma'aunin W'
  • Labarai mai daɗi: FTP da CP yawanci sun kasance cikin ±5W na juna. Masu hawa da yawa suna amfani da FTP don sauƙi, suna sanin CP zai ba da yankunan iri ɗaya

FTP da Training Stress Score (TSS)

FTP shine mai ragi wanda ke mai yiwuwa ƙididdigar Training Stress Score (TSS), yana ba da damar ƙididdigar nauyin horo ta hanyar bayanai.

Yadda TSS ke Amfani da FTP

Training Stress Score yana ƙididdige nauyin horo na kowane hawa ta hanyar haɗa zafi da tsawon lokaci:

Lissafin TSS

TSS = (daƙiƙoƙi × NP × IF) / (FTP × 3600) × 100

Inda:

  • NP = Normalized Power (matsakaicin auna da ke lissafin canjin)
  • IF = Intensity Factor (NP / FTP)
  • FTP = Functional Threshold Power naku

Mai Sauƙi:

TSS = (IF)² × tsawon lokaci (sa'o'i) × 100

Sa'a guda a FTP = TSS 100

Misalin Lissafin TSS

Bayanan Hawa:

  • Tsawon lokaci: Sa'a 2 (daƙiƙoƙi 7,200)
  • Normalized Power: 210W
  • FTP naku: 250W

Mataki na 1: Lissafin Intensity Factor

IF = NP / FTP
IF = 210W / 250W
IF = 0.84

Mataki na 2: Lissafin TSS

TSS = (0.84)² × sa'a 2 × 100
TSS = 0.706 × 2 × 100
TSS = 141 TSS

Fassara: Wannan hawa na jimiri na sa'a 2 a 84% na FTP ya ƙirƙiri nauyin horo daidai da sa'a 1.41 a iyaka. Yana da ingantaccen horo na aerobic wanda zai ba da gudunmawa ga lafiya ba tare da gajiya mai yawa ba.

Me ya sa FTP Daidai Yake da Mahimmanci don TSS

Idan FTP naku ya yi ƙasa jùwa, TSS zai fi yawa da wucin gadi, yana sa ku yi tunani kuna horo da wahala fiye da yadda kuke. Idan FTP ya yi sama jùwa, TSS zai kasance ƙasa, wanda zai iya haifar da gajiya mai yawa saboda kun yi ƙanƙanta yawan gajiyarku. FTP daidai = bin diddigin nauyin horo daidai.

FTP na Hanya vs MTB: Bambance-bambance Masu Mahimmanci

Ƙimar FTP na hanya da keke na dutse sun bambanta sosai saboda bambancin biomechanics, tsarin cadence, da bambancin bayarwa na iko.

🚴 FTP na Hawan Keke na Hanya

  • Iko mafi girma na cikakke: Ƙoƙarori mai daidaituwa na dogon lokaci
  • Cadence mafi kyau: 85-95 RPM a iyaka
  • Bayarwa mai laushi: VI (Variability Index) na 1.02-1.05
  • Matsayi na aerodynamic: Ƙasa, matsayi mai tsanani
  • Ƙoƙarori masu tsawo na dogon lokaci: Bukukuwan iyaka na minti 20-60

🚵 FTP na MTB

  • Iko 5-10% ƙasa: Saboda buƙatun fasaha da matsayi
  • Cadence mai canjawa: Matsakaicin 70-85 RPM, canje-canje akai-akai
  • Iko mai fasaha: VI na 1.10-1.20+ tare da fasahar akai-akai
  • Matsayi mai tsaye: Yana raunana ikon don sarrafa keke
  • Ƙoƙarori mai canzawa-canzawa: Farfadowar micro akai-akai akan sassa na fasaha

⚠️ Me ya sa FTP na MTB ya fi ƙasa

Masu hawan keke na dutse suna fuskantar:

  • Matsayi na jiki: Matsayi mai tsaye don sarrafa fasaha yana rage ingancin canja iko
  • Canjin cadence: Hanzarori akai-akai da sassa na fasaha suna rikitar da zagawa
  • Asarar dakarkari: Kekuna masu cikakken suspension suna sha 14-30% na iko a ƙasa mai wahala
  • Haɗa tsoka: Shigar da sama na jiki don sarrafa keke yana ɗaukar makamashi daga ƙafafu
  • Canjin ƙasa: Duwatsu, tushe, da siffofi na fasaha suna buƙatar modulation iko akai-akai

✅ Bin Diddigin Ƙimar FTP daban-daban

Bike Analytics yana bin diddigin ƙimar FTP daban-daban ta atomatik don hanya da MTB. Ƙa'idojin gwaji:

  • FTP na Hanya: Gwada a hanya mai lebur ko mai horo na cikin gida tare da iko mai daidaituwa na dogon lokaci
  • FTP na MTB: Gwada a hanyar hawa ta matsakaici ko mai horo na cikin gida a matsayin MTB
  • Bambance da ake tsammani: FTP na MTB yawanci 5-10% ƙasa da FTP na hanya

W/kg vs Watts na Cikakke

Ma'aunin iko-nauyi (W/kg) yana da ma'ana fiye da watts na cikakke don hawan tudu da kwatanta masu hawa daban-daban:

  • Hawan tudu: W/kg yana hango kai tsaye gudu na hawan tudu (iyakance ta nauyi)
  • Ƙasa mai lebur: Watts na cikakke yana fi mahimmanci (iyakance ta aerodynamics)
  • Gwajin lokaci: Ikon cikakke + aerodynamics sun fi W/kg muhimmanci

Lissafin W/kg naku: FTP (watts) / nauyin jiki (kg)

Tabbacin Kimiyya na FTP

Allen & Coggan (2019) - Training and Racing with a Power Meter

Dr. Andrew Coggan da Hunter Allen sun kafa FTP a matsayin ma'aunin tushe don horo na tushen iko a cikin aikin su na farko, yanzu a bitan 3:

  • Ma'anar iyaka mai amfani: Mafi girman ikon sa'a 1 ba tare da tarawa gajiya ba
  • Tsarin gwajin minti 20: 95% na ikon minti 20 yana daidaita sosai da ikon minti 60
  • Yana ba da damar Training Stress Score: Ƙididdigewa ta hanyar bayanai na nauyin horo
  • Yankunan horo na keɓaɓɓu: Tsarin yankunan 7 dangane da iyakoki na ilimin jiki
  • Ma'aunin masana'antu: An karɓa ta TrainingPeaks, Zwift, TrainerRoad, da dukkan dandamali masu muhimmanci

MacInnis, Thomas & Phillips (2019) - Amincin Gwajin FTP

Binciken tabbatarwa da ke nuna amincin da ake iya maimaita gwajin FTP a masu horo:

  • Aminci mai girma: ICC = 0.98, r² = 0.96 tsakanin gwajin sake
  • Iyakar iya maimaita: ±2W son wajen, kuskuren yau da kullum 2.3%
  • Yana gano ikon sa'a 1: Yana hango iyakar da ake iya dorewa daidai a 89% na masu horo
  • Madadin mai amfani: Madadin da yake aiki don gwajin lactate na dakin gwaje-gwaje mai tsada

Madogara: MacInnis, M.J., Thomas, A.C.Q., & Phillips, S.M. (2019). "Is the FTP Test a Reliable, Reproducible and Functional Assessment Tool in Highly-Trained Athletes?" International Journal of Exercise Science, PMC6886609.

Karsten et al. (2019) - Ingancin FTP don Hasashen Aiki

Bincike da ke nuna fifikon FTP akan VO₂max don hango aikin hawan keke:

  • Daidaitawar aiki mai ƙarfi: W/kg a FTP yana daidaita da aikin tsere (r = -0.74, p < 0.01)
  • Mafi kyau da VO₂max: VO₂max bai nuna mahimman daidaituwa ba (r = -0.37)
  • Amfani mai amfani: FTP yana fassara kai tsaye zuwa gudu na tsere da tsarin horo

Madogara: Karsten, B., et al. (2019). "The Validity of Functional Threshold Power and Maximal Oxygen Uptake for Cycling Performance in Moderately Trained Cyclists" PMC6835290.

🔬 Me ya sa FTP ke Aiki

Functional Threshold Power yana wakiltar iyaka tsakanin muhallan motsa jiki na nauyi da mai tsanani. A ƙasa FTP, samarwa da share lactate suna kasancewa cikin daidaituwa—zaku iya dorewa ba tare da ƙarewa ba (a ka'idar). A sama FTP, lactate yana taruwa a hankali har zuwa ƙarewa cikin minti 20-60.

Wannan yana sa FTP ya zama zafi na cikakke don:

  • Saita gudu mai ɗorewar gwajin lokaci da hawan tudu
  • Tsarawa horo na intervals na kofar lactate
  • Sa ido akan ingantuwar lafiya na aerobic akan lokaci
  • Lissafin nauyin horo, gajiya, da buƙatun farfadowa

Tambayoyi da Ake Yawan Yi game da FTP

Mene ne FTP a hawan keke?

FTP (Functional Threshold Power) shine mafi girman matsakaicin ikon da zaku iya dorewa na kimanin sa'a guda ba tare da tara gajiya mai yawa ba. Yana wakiltar kofar aerobic dinku—zafin da samarwa lactate take daidai da share lactate. FTP yana zama tushen yankunan horo na tushen iko na keɓaɓɓu da ƙididdigar nauyin horo daidai.

Yaya ake gwada FTP nawa?

Tsarin gwajin FTP da ake amfani da shi fiye: (1) Dumi-dumi minti 20, (2) Hawa dukkan ƙoƙari na minti 5, (3) Hutawa minti 10, (4) Hawa mafi girman ƙoƙari mai ɗorewar minti 20 kuma rubuta matsakaicin ikon, (5) Lissafi FTP = 95% na matsakaicin ikon minti 20. Hanyoyi madadin sun haɗa da gwajin ramp (75% na mafi girman ikon minti 1) ko ainihin gwajin minti 60 (mafi daidaituwa amma mai wahala sosai).

Sau nawa ya kamata in sake gwada FTP nawa?

Sake gwada FTP naku kowane mako 6-8 a lokacin bukukuwan horo mai aiki don sabunta yankunan horo yayin da lafiyar ku ta inganta. Gwada sau da yawa (kowane mako 4) a lokacin matakin ginin mai tsanani, ko lokacin da kuke iya wuce yankunan iko da aka tsara akai-akai. Kuma sake gwada bayan manyan canje-canje na lafiya (rashin lafiya, rauni, lokacin hutun kakar), kafin fara sabbin shirye-shiryen horo, ko lokacin da bugun zuciya a ikon iyaka ya ragu sosai.

Mene ne FTP mai kyau don mai hawan keke na farko?

Don masu farawa tare da ƙarancin shekara 1 na horo mai tsari, FTP na yau da kullum shine 2.0-2.5 W/kg (140-175W don mai hawa na kg 70). Masu hawa amatir suna horo sa'a 5-8/mako yawanci suna cimma 2.5-3.5 W/kg. Kada ku kwatanta kanku da ƙwararru (5.5-6.5 W/kg)—mayar da hankali kan inganta FTP naku da 10-20% akan kakar horo. Duk FTP shine farkon wurin da ake iya fara horo na tushen iko.

FTP ɗaya ne da Critical Power?

FTP da Critical Power (CP) suna da kusanci amma sun bambanta. FTP lamba ɗaya ce da ke wakiltar ikon sa'a 1 naku, yayin da CP wani ɓangare ne na tsarin ɓangarori biyu (CP + ƙarfin anaerobic W'). Yawanci suna bambanta kawai ±5W, tare da CP yawanci 5-7W mafi girma da FTP. FTP yana da sauƙi kuma mafi amfani don yawancin masu hawa, yayin da CP yana ba da samfurar daidai na ƙoƙarori masu canjawa-zafi kuma yana ba da damar bin diddigin ma'aunin W' don yanayin tsere.

Me ya sa FTP na cikin gida ya fi ƙasa da waje?

FTP na cikin gida yawanci 5-10W ƙasa da waje saboda taruwar zafi, rashin sanyaya iska, da abubuwan tunani. A cikin gida, yanayin zafi na jikin ku yana tashi da sauri ba tare da sanyin iska na halitta ba, yana tilasta ku rage ikon don hana zafi mai yawa. Matsayin tsaye akan mai horo kuma yana rage ingancin haɗa tsoka. Don horo daidai, yi amfani da ƙimar FTP daban-daban don hawa na cikin gida da waje, ko gwada a muhalli inda kuka yi yawancin horonku.

Mene ne bambance tsakanin FTP na hanya da MTB?

FTP na MTB yawanci 5-10% ƙasa da FTP na hanya saboda: (1) matsayin hawa mai tsaye yana rage ingancin canja iko, (2) dakarkari yana sha 14-30% na iko a ƙasa mai wahala, (3) canjin cadence daga sassa na fasaha, (4) shigar da sama na jiki don sarrafa keke. Zan iya kiyasin FTP ba tare da gwaji ba?

Yayin da zaku iya kiyasin FTP daga bayanan tsere na kwanan nan ko hawaye masu wahala na ƙungiya, gwaji kai tsaye ya fi daidaituwa sosai don lissafin TSS da tsarin yankin horo. Kiyasin daga ikon tsere: yi amfani da 95-100% na hawa mai ƙarfi na minti 40-60 ko ƙoƙarin gwajin lokaci. Duk da haka, gwajin minti 20 yana ɗaukar minti 50 kawai lapapọ̀ (haɗa dumi-dumi) kuma yana ba da daidaituwar da ake buƙata don horo mai inganci na tushen iko.
