Gudanar da Nauyin Horo: TSS, CTL, ATL & TSB don Hawan Keke

Koyi Performance Management Chart don inganta horo, hana gajiya mai yawa, da samun kololuwar burin ku na hawan keke

🎯 Ma'aurata Masu Mahimmanci: Nauyin Horo don Hawan Keke

  • Training Stress Score (TSS) yana auna yadda kowane hawa yake shafar jikin ku, yana haɗa iko, zafi, da tsawon lokaci
  • CTL (Chronic Training Load) yana auna lafiyar ku ta dogon lokaci wanda aka gina akan kwanaki 42 na horo mai tsauri
  • ATL (Acute Training Load) yana bin diddigin gajiya ta kwanan nan daga kwanaki 7 da suka wuce na hawa
  • TSB (Training Stress Balance) yana nuna ma'aunin lafiya-gajiya naku da shirye-shirye don tsere ko hutawa
  • Fahimtar nauyin horo na hawan keke yana hana gajiya mai yawa kuma yana inganta lokacin aiki ta hanyar tsarawa ta bayanai

Tushe: Lissafin TSS yana buƙatar Functional Threshold Power (FTP) naku a matsayin matakin iyaka.

Mene ne Training Stress Score (TSS)?

Training Stress Score yana ba da amsar tambayar mahimmanci: Yaya hawan nan yake da wahala? Ba kawai nisa ko lokaci ba, amma ainihin damuwar ilimin jiki da ake dora wa jikin ku ta kowane zaman horo na hawan keke.

Training Stress Score (TSS), wanda Dr. Andrew Coggan ya haɓaka, yana ba da hanyar daidaitacciyar auna zafi da tsawon lokacin horo zuwa lamba ɗaya. TSS ya juya horo na tushen iko zuwa samun damar yin amfani da shi.

Ƙa'idar TSS don Hawan Keke

Sa'a guda a Functional Threshold Power (FTP) naku = 100 TSS

Wannan daidaitawa yana ba da damar kwatanta akan hawa, makonni, da zagayen horo. Ƙoƙarin minti 30 na iyaka = ~50 TSS. Hawa na sa'a 2 na iyaka = ~200 TSS.

Yadda Ake Lissafin TSS

TSS = (daƙiƙoƙi × NP × IF) / (FTP × 3600) × 100

Inda:

  • NP (Normalized Power / Ikon da aka Daidaita) = "Farashi" na ilimin jiki na hawa
  • IF (Intensity Factor / Faratsa na Zafi) = NP / FTP (zafi dangane da iyaka)
  • Tsawon lokaci = Jimlar lokacin hawa cikin daƙiƙoƙi
  • FTP = Functional threshold power naku cikin watts

Misali: Hawa na Sa'a 2 na Jimiri

Bayanin Mai Hawa:

  • FTP: 250W

Bayanan Hawa:

  • Tsawon lokaci: Sa'a 2 (daƙiƙoƙi 7200)
  • Normalized Power: 200W

Mataki na 1: Lissafin Intensity Factor (IF)

IF = NP / FTP
IF = 200W / 250W
IF = 0.80

Mataki na 2: Lissafin TSS

TSS = (7200s × 200W × 0.80) / (250W × 3600) × 100
TSS = (1,152,000) / (900,000) × 100
TSS = 128 TSS

Fassara: Wannan hawa na jimiri na sa'a 2 a 80% na FTP ya samar da TSS 128 — ƙarfin ƙarfafawa na aerobic wanda yafi dacewa da hawaye na ginin tushe.

📊 Manufofin TSS na Mako-mako ta Matakin Mai Hawan Keke

  • Mai Hawa na Farko: 200-400 TSS/mako (hawaye 3-4/mako)
  • Mai Hawa Amatir: 400-600 TSS/mako (hawaye 4-5/mako)
  • Mai Hawa na Gasa Amatir: 600-900 TSS/mako (hawaye 5-7/mako)
  • Manazarci/Ƙwararru: 900-1500+ TSS/mako (zaman 8-12+/mako)

Waɗannan suna taruwa zuwa Chronic Training Load (CTL) naku, ma'aunin lafiya da aka bayyana a ƙasa.

Performance Management Chart (PMC)

PMC yana nuna ma'aunai uku masu haɗin kai waɗanda ke ba da cikakkiyar labarin horo naku na hawan keke: lafiya, gajiya, da form.

📈

CTL - Chronic Training Load

LAFIYAR KU (FITNESS)

Matsakaicin nauyin yau da kullum da aka auna akan kwanaki 42 na TSS. Yana wakiltar lafiyar aerobic ta dogon lokaci da daidaituwar horo daga hawa mai tsauri.

CTL yau = CTL jiya + (TSS yau - CTL jiya) / 42
⚡

ATL - Acute Training Load

GAJIYAR KU (FATIGUE)

Matsakaicin nauyin yau da kullum da aka auna akan kwanaki 7 na TSS. Yana kama damuwar horo ta kwanan nan da gajiyar da aka tara daga mako ɗaya na hawa.

ATL yau = ATL jiya + (TSS yau - ATL jiya) / 7
🎯

TSB - Training Stress Balance

FORM NAKU (SHIRYE-SHIRYE)

Banbance tsakanin lafiyar jiya (CTL) da gajiyar yau (ATL). Yana nuna shirye-shirye don yin aiki ko buƙatar hutawa kafin hawa ko tsere mai inganci.

TSB = CTL (jiya) - ATL (yau)

CTL: Ma'aunin Lafiyar Hawan Keke Naku

Abin da CTL Ya Wakilta don Masu Hawan Keke

CTL yana ƙididdige nauyin horo wanda jikin ku ya daidaita zuwa a cikin makonni 6 da suka wuce. CTL mafi girma a hawan keke na nufin:

  • Yawan ƙarfin aerobic da jimiri
  • Ikon ɗaukar ƙarin yawan horo da zafi
  • Ingantaccen ƙarfin sinadarai da konewar mai
  • Mafi girman ikon da za a iya riƙewa
  • Farfadowa mafi kyau tsakanin ƙoƙarori masu wahala

Lokaci na Daidaituwa: Kwanaki 42

CTL yana da rabin rayuwa na kimanin kwanaki 14-15. Bayan kwanaki 42, kusan 36.8% (1/e) na tasirin horon ɗaya ya rage a cikin lissafin lafiyar ku.

Wannan lalacewa mai jinkiri na nufin lafiya yana ginin a hankali amma kuma yana raguwa a hankali — zaku iya yin mako guda hutawa ba tare da asarar lafiya mai yawa ba.

Daidaitattun Ƙimar CTL ta Matakin Mai Hawan Keke

Masu Hawa Amatir:

Horo na yau da kullum, hawaye 4-5/mako

⚠️ Iyakokin Ramp Rate na CTL
  • Aminci: +3-8 CTL a mako
  • Mai tsanani amma mai dorewa: +8-12 CTL a mako
  • Hadarin da yawa: >12 CTL a mako

Wuce waɗannan iyakokin yana ƙara haɗarin rauni da rashin lafiya sosai. Gina a hankali don samun fa'idar lafiya mai dorewa.

ATL: Ma'aunin Gajiyar Hawan Keke Naku

ATL yana bin diddigin damuwar horo na gajeren lokaci — gajiyar da aka tara a cikin mako ɗaya na hawa. Yana tashi da sauri bayan horo mai wahala kuma yana faɗuwa da sauri a lokacin hutawa, yana mai yiwuwa sarrafa farfadowa tsakanin zaman masu inganci.

Yadda ATL Ke Aiki a Horo na Hawan Keke

  • Saurin Amsa: Lokaci na daidaituwa na kwanaki 7 (rabin rayuwa ~kwanaki 2.4)
  • Tsarin Tashi da Faɗuwa: Yana tashi bayan hawaye masu wahala, yana faɗuwa a lokacin kwanakin hutawa
  • Alƙalamin Farfadowa: ATL mai faɗuwa = gajiya mai tafiya tsakanin zaman
  • Gargadin Gajiya mai yawa: ATL mai kara da yawa yana nuna farfadowa mara isasshe
  • Ma'aunin Horo: ATL ya kamata ya bi CTL amma ya bambanta ƙari

🔬 Tsarin Lafiya-Gajiya

Kowane hawa na horo yana samar da sakamakon biyu:

  1. Ƙarfafawar lafiya (mai ginin a hankali, mai dawwama)
  2. Gajiya (mai ginin da sauri, mai tafiya da sauri)

Aiki = Lafiya - Gajiya. PMC yana nuna wannan tsarin, yana ba da damar tsarawa ta kimiyya da lokacin tsere mafi kyau.

A Lokacin Daidaituwa

Idan nauyin horo ya daidaitu daga mako zuwa mako, CTL da ATL suna haɗewa:

Misali: 500 TSS/mako akai-akai

TSS na yau da kullum ≈ 71
CTL yana kusa da ~71
ATL yana kusa da ~71
TSB yana kusa da 0

Fassara: Lafiya da gajiya sun daidaitu. Kulawa mai dorewa ta horo — babu tarawa ta rashi ko ƙarin.

A Lokacin Matakin Ginin

Idan aka ƙara yawa ko zafi:

ATL yana tashi da sauri fiye da CTL saboda lokacin daidaituwa mafi gajeren. TSB ya zama mara kyau (gajiya > lafiya). Wannan al'ada ne kuma mai amfani — kuna sanya nauyi mai yawa don samar da daidaituwa.

Manufar TSB: -10 zuwa -30 a lokacin bukukuwan horo masu amfani

A Lokacin Taper don Tsere

Idan aka rage nauyin horo kafin gasa:

ATL yana faɗuwa da sauri fiye da CTL. TSB yana zama mai kyau (lafiya > gajiya). Wannan shine manufar — zo ranar tsere sabo tare da ƙafafu masu shirye kuma an riƙe lafiya.

Manufar TSB: +10 zuwa +25 a ranar tsere dangane da abin da ake buƙata

TSB: Ma'aunin Lafiya-Gajiya Naku da Shirye-Shirye don Tsere

TSB (Training Stress Balance) shine bambance tsakanin lafiyar jiya (CTL) da gajiyar yau (ATL). Yana nuna ko kun kasance sabo ko gaji, shirye don tsere ko kuna buƙatar kwanakin hutawa.

🎯 Manufar TSB ta Nau'in Abin da Ake Buƙata

  • Criterium/Gudu: TSB +5 zuwa +15 (taper gajere, riƙe kaifi)
  • Tsere na Hanya: TSB +10 zuwa +20 (taper kwanaki 10-14)
  • Time Trials: TSB +15 zuwa +25 (taper kwanaki 14-21 don mafi girman iko)
  • Gran Fondos/Centuries: TSB +5 zuwa +15 (taper kwanaki 7-10, riƙe jimiri)
  • Tsere na Mataki: TSB -5 zuwa +10 (zo da ɗan gajiya, ana buƙatar jimiri fiye da sabonsa)

Gajeren tsare mafi fasaha suna buƙatar TSB mafi girma. Tsare jimiri mai tsawo suna buƙatar TSB mai matsakaici don riƙe jimiri.

Tsarawa da Performance Management Chart

Misali na Zagayen Horo na Makonni 16

Makonni 1-4: Mataki na Tushe

  • Manufa: Ginin CTL a hankali a +3-5 maki/mako
  • TSS na Mako-mako: 400 → 450 → 500 → 450 (mako na hutawa)
  • Ci gaban CTL: 60 → 73
  • Kewayen TSB: -5 zuwa -15 (gajiya mai sarrafa)
  • Kulawa: Hawaye masu tsawo da daidaituwa, jimiri na Yanki 2, ginin tushen aerobic

Makonni 5-8: Mataki na Ginin

  • Manufa: Ci gaba da haɓakar CTL a +5-8 maki/mako tare da horo mafi wahala
  • TSS na Mako-mako: 500 → 550 → 600 → 500 (mako na hutawa)
  • Ci gaban CTL: 73 → 93
  • Kewayen TSB: -15 zuwa -25 (nauyi mai amfani)
  • Kulawa: Sweet spot intervals, aiki na iyaka, hawaye masu tsawo na tempo

Makonni 9-12: Mataki na Kololuwa

  • Manufa: Haɓaka CTL zuwa kololuwa, mafi girman nauyin horo
  • TSS na Mako-mako: 600 → 650 → 650 → 550 (mako na hutawa)
  • Ci gaban CTL: 93 → 108
  • Kewayen TSB: -20 zuwa -30 (mafi girman ƙarfafawa)
  • Kulawa: Intervals takamaiman tsere, VO₂max, kwaikwayon tsere

Makonni 13-14: Mataki na Hutawa

  • Manufa: Bari jiki ya sha horo, shirya don taper
  • TSS na Mako-mako: 400 → 400
  • Ci gaban CTL: 108 → 103 (ɗan raguwa, an riƙe lafiya)
  • Kewayen TSB: -5 zuwa +5 (tsaka-tsaki)
  • Kulawa: Jimiri mai sauƙi, hawaye masu daɗi, rage zafi

Makonni 15-16: Taper + Mako na Tsere

  • Manufa: Kololuwa don ranar tsere tare da TSB +15-20
  • TSS na Mako-mako: 350 → 250 + tsere
  • Ci gaban CTL: 103 → 98 (ɗan asara mara yawa)
  • Ci gaban ATL: Yana faɗuwa da sauri daga ~85 zuwa ~50
  • TSB a ranar tsere:+18 zuwa +22
  • Sakamakon: Ƙafafu masu sabo, an riƙe lafiya, shirye don kololuwar aiki

✅ Me ya sa Taper ke Aiki

Lokuttan daidaituwa daban-daban (kwanaki 42 don CTL, kwanaki 7 don ATL) suna ƙirƙirar tasirin taper:

  • ATL yana amsa da sauri → Gajiya ta ɓace cikin kwanaki 7-10
  • CTL yana amsa a hankali → Lafiya ta dawwama makonni
  • Sakamakon: Lafiya ta rage yayin da gajiya ta ɓace = ƙafafu masu sabo don kololuwar aiki

Abubuwan da Suka Dace da MTB

Hawan keke na dutse yana buƙatar fassarar PMC daban-daban:

  • Hanya guda, TSS daban-daban: Wahalar hanya yana shafar TSS sosai don hanya guda
  • Gano fasahar: Ƙidaya fasahar da suka wuce iyaka — tsare XC na iya samun 88+ cikin sa'a 2
  • Ƙwarewar fasaha ta fi mahimmanci: Ikon fasaha yana lissafin ~50% na aiki na MTB vs ~20% a hawan hanya
  • Yi amfani da gajeren taguwar iko: Matsakaicin daƙiƙa 3-5 suna da mahimmanci don MTB vs daƙiƙa 30 don hanya

Kuskuren Sarrafa Nauyin Horo da ake Sawa

1️⃣ Ginin CTL da Sauri jùwa

Kuskure: Tsalle 15-20 maki na CTL a mako ɗaya yana ƙoƙarin "kama" matakin lafiya da aka yi niyya.

Mafita: Iyakance ƙara zuwa 3-8 CTL/mako. Sauranta — lafiya yana ɗaukar lokaci don gina da aminci.

2️⃣ Rashin Yin Taper

Kuskure: Horo mai tsanani akai-akai, isar da tsare tare da TSB -20 zuwa -30.

Mafita: Rage yawa kwanaki 7-14 kafin manyan abubuwa. Bari TSB ya tashi zuwa +10-25 don kololuwar aiki.

3️⃣ Yin Watsi da Gargaɗin TSB

Kuskure: Ci gaba da tura yayin da TSB ya faɗi ƙasa da -30 na tsawon lokaci.

Mafita: Ɗauki hutawa ta tilas yayin da TSB < -30. Jiki naku yana gaya muku yana buƙatar hutawa.

4️⃣ Bin Hawaye Mara Tsari

Kuskure: Bin abin da yake da daɗi a wancan lokaci maimakon ƙara nauyi mai ci gaba.

Mafita: Bi shirin da aka tsara tare da ci gaban CTL da aka tsara da makonni na hutawa.

5️⃣ Kwatanta da Wasu

Kuskure: "Abokin gasa nawa yana da CTL 120, ina buƙata haka!"

Mafita: CTL na kowane mutum ne. CTL mai dorewa naku ya dogara ga tarihin horo ku da kwayoyin halitta.

6️⃣ Tsallake Makonni na Hutawa

Kuskure: Ginin CTL akai-akai ba tare da makonni na hutawa da aka tsara ba.

Mafita: Kowace makonni 3-4, rage yawa 30-40% don haɗa daidaituwar lafiya.

Tambayoyi da Ake Yawan Yi: Nauyin Horo don Hawan Keke

Nawa TSS a mako ya kamata in yi?

TSS na mako-mako ya dogara ga matakin ku: Masu farawa: 200-400 TSS/mako, Amatir: 400-600 TSS/mako, Mai gasa amatir: 600-900 TSS/mako, Ƙwararru: 900-1500+ TSS/mako. Fara a hankali kuma kara a hankali da 3-8 maki na CTL a mako.

Mene ne CTL mai kyau don matakin hawan keke nawa?

Daidaitattun ƙimar CTL: Masu farawa 20-40, Masu hawa na yau da kullum 40-80, Amatir na gasa 80-120, Ƙwararru 120-200+. CTL mai dorewa naku ya dogara ga tarihin horo, lokacin da ake samu, da ikon farfadowa. Mayar da hankali kan ci gaban ci gaba maimakon lambobi mafi girma.

Tsawon lokaci nawa ya kamata in yi taper kafin tsere?

Tsawon lokacin taper ya dogara ga nau'in tsere: Gajeren tsare/criteriums: kwanaki 7-10, Tsare na hanya: kwanaki 10-14, Time trials: kwanaki 14-21 (ana buƙatar mafi girman sabo), Tsare na mataki: kwanaki 7-10 (ba ku so ku kasance sabo sosai). Manufar TSB +10 zuwa +25 a ranar tsere dangane da buƙatun tsare.

Zan iya horo yayin da TSB ke ƙasa da -30?

TSB ƙasa da -30 yana nuna gajiya mai tsanani da haɗarin gajiya mai yawa. Ba a shawarci ci gaba da horo mai wahala a wannan matakin ba. Rage yawa 50%+ kuma yi hawaye masu sauƙi na farfadowa har sai TSB ya tashi zuwa -20 ko mafi girma. Saurari jiki naku — gajiya mai dawwama, raguwar iko, bacci mara kyau, ko rashin lafiya sune alamu.

Yaya ake amfani da PMC don tseren keke na dutse (MTB)?

Tsaren MTB yana buƙatar fassarar PMC da aka canza: Mai da hankali kan gano fasaha/sauri sama da iyaka (88+ a tsare XC), yi amfani da gajeren taguwar iko (daƙiƙa 3-5), gane cewa ƙwarewar fasaha ta lissafa ~50% na aiki (vs ~20% hawan hanya), kuma fahimci cewa wahalar hanya yana shafar tarawa ta TSS sosai don hanya guda.

Me ya sa CTL ke raguwa a lokacin taper?

CTL mai raguwa ɗan ɗan a lokacin taper al'ada ne kuma ana tsammani. Raguwar CTL maki 5-10 akan makonni 2 na taper yana wakiltar asarar lafiya mara yawa (lokaci na daidaituwa na kwanaki 42 na nufin an riƙe lafiya). Yayin da haka, ATL yana faɗuwa da sauri sosai (lokaci na daidaituwa na kwanaki 7), yana kawar da gajiya. Wannan yana ƙirƙirar TSB mai kyau da ƙafafu masu sabo don tsere.

Mene ne bambance tsakanin TSS da kilojoules?

Kilojoules yana auna jimlar aiki da aka yi (kashe makamashi), yayin da TSS yana auna damuwar horo dangane da FTP naku. Hawa na 200W na sa'a 1 = 720 kJ ga kowa, amma TSS ya dogara ga FTP naku. Idan FTP naku shine 200W, TSS = 100. Idan FTP naku shine 300W, TSS = 44. TSS yana lissafin matakin lafiya na kowane mutum; kJ baya yin haka.

Shin ya kamata in bi TSS don horo na cikin gida?

Tabbas. TSS ya fi dacewa don horo na cikin gida saboda bayanai na iko sun daidaitu kuma an sarrafa masu canjin yanayi. TSS na cikin gida yana ba da gudunmawa kai tsaye ga lissafin CTL/ATL/TSB naku. Duk da haka, wasu masu hawa suna samun suna iya ɗaukar ɗan ƙarin TSS na cikin gida fiye da waje saboda babu tsayawa ko sauka.

Mallaki Nauyin Horo na Hawan Keke Naku

Fahimtar Training Stress Score da Performance Management Chart yana canza horo na son rai zuwa ingantaccen aiki na tushen bayanai. Ta hanyar sa ido kan TSS, CTL, ATL, da TSB, kuna samun cikakkiyar ikon sarrafa ci gaban lafiya, sarrafa gajiya, da lokacin tsere na kololuwa.

Tsarin CTL-ATL-TSB yana hana gajiya mai yawa, inganta lokacin farfadowa, kuma yana tabbatar da isar da tsare mahimmanku tare da daidaitaccen ma'aunin lafiya-gajiya don kololuwar aiki na hawan keke.
