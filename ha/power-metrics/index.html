Ma'aunin Wutar Keke: NP, IF, VI, da W'bal Yayi Bayani

Hanya mai amfani don haɗa fayilolin tafiya zuwa Saukewa: FTP, Mahimman Ƙarfi, yankunan horo, yanke shawara, da kuma Farashin TSS

Amsa da sauri

Ma'aunin wutar lantarki juya danyen watts cikin yanke shawara. Ƙarfin Ƙarfi (NP) yana ƙididdige gaskiyar farashin hawan canji, Factor mai ƙarfi (IF) ya nuna irin wahalar da hawan ya kasance Saukewa: FTP, Fihirisar Sauyawa (VI) yana nuna yadda ƙoƙarin ya tsaya ko hargitsi, kuma W'bal yana taimakawa wajen fassara yawan ƙarfin sama da ƙasa da kuka kashe.

  • Mafi amfani: takardar sayan magani, tseren tsere, da kuma binciken bayan hawa
  • Mafi amfani anka: wani sabon zamani Saukewa: FTP ko Mahimman Ƙarfi daraja
  • Babban kuskure: kwatanta NP, IF, ko W'bal ba tare da mahallin yanayi ba, tsawon lokaci, da nau'in taron.
  • Mafi kyawun mataki na gaba: haɗa ma'auni zuwa yankunan horo kuma Farashin TSS maimakon karanta su a ware

  • NP ya gaya muku dalilin da yasa hawa biyu tare da matsakaicin matsakaici iri ɗaya na iya jin daban

Yawancin masu keke ba sa buƙatar ƙarin awo. Suna buƙatar ingantacciyar hanya don fassara waɗanda ke cikin fayil ɗin hawan. Wannan shafin shine cibiyar aiyuka na wannan tsarin: abin da ma'aunin ma'aunin wutar lantarki ke nufi, lokacin da suke taimakawa, inda suka gaza, da kuma waɗanne shafuka masu alaƙa ya kamata ku yi amfani da su na gaba.

Takaitacciyar Ma'auni-by-Metric

Ƙarfin Ƙarfi (NP)

NP yana ƙididdige farashin abin hawa bayan hauhawar nauyi fiye da tsayayyen hawa. Yana da mahimmanci don fassarar tafiye-tafiye masu wahala, tseren tsere, da fayilolin MTB na fasaha inda matsakaicin iko ke ɓoye ainihin nau'in.

Factor mai ƙarfi (IF)

Saukewa: IF ita ce hanya mafi sauƙi don sanya tafiya akan sikelin kofa. Yana zama da amfani kawai lokacin da naka Saukewa: FTP abin yarda ne. Idan FTP ba ta da kyau, IF ya zama daidaitaccen kuskure.

Fihirisar Sauyawa (VI)

VI ya gaya muku ko ƙoƙarin ya tsaya ko ya rikice. A cikin gwaji na lokaci, ƙananan VI yawanci yana da kyau taki. A cikin tseren XC ko ma'auni, VI mafi girma zai iya nuna kawai buƙatun taron.

W'bal

W'bal yana taimaka wa mahaya mai tsanani fassara maimaita aiki a sama Mahimman Ƙarfi. Yana da matukar amfani idan aka haɗa su tare da mahallin tsere, ba azaman lambar dashboard ɗin duniya don kowane abin hawa ba.

Tambayoyin da ake yawan yi

Me yasa NP sau da yawa ya fi matsakaicin ƙarfi?

Saboda matsananciyar hawan jini yana haifar da rashin daidaituwar farashin ilimin lissafi. NP yana ɗaukar nauyin hawan da yawa, don haka tafiya mai canzawa yawanci yakan yi wuya ta hanyar NP fiye da matsakaicin iko kadai.

Menene VI mai kyau don hawan keke?

Babu VI mai kyau na duniya. A time trial may target roughly 1.00-1.03, while a criterium or XC race can naturally sit much higher. Alkali VI akan taron, ba ma'auni ɗaya na duniya ba.

Ina bukatan Ƙarfi mai mahimmanci idan na riga na yi amfani da FTP?

Ba koyaushe ba. FTP ya isa ga mahayan da yawa. Ƙarfin Mahimmanci yana zama mafi amfani lokacin da kake kula da maimaita aikin sama-kofa, W'bal, da mafi ƙaƙƙarfan ƙira na ƙoƙari mai tsanani.

Sau nawa zan sabunta waɗannan ma'aunin?

Sake gwadawa ko sake ƙididdige madaidaicin ƙofa kowane mako 6-8 ko bayan shingen horo mai ma'ana. Ma'auni suna da amfani kawai kamar ƙimar ƙofa a bayansu.
