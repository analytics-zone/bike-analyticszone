Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 27, 2025

Your Privacy is Protected

BikeAnalytics does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All data processing happens locally on your device.

Overview

BikeAnalytics is designed with your privacy as a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to protecting your personal information and data.

In short: We don't collect your data because we don't need it. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

BikeAnalytics does not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Cycling activity data (distance, power, speed, times, etc.)
  • Health data (heart rate, calories, etc.)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Crash reports or diagnostics
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

How the App Works

All calculations and data processing in BikeAnalytics happen entirely on your device:

  • Your cycling data is stored only in your device's local storage
  • All calculations (power, FTP, TSS, zones, etc.) are performed locally
  • No data is ever sent to our servers or any third-party services
  • No internet connection is required for the app to function

Data Storage

Any data you enter or generate in the app is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage mechanisms. This data remains under your complete control:

  • Your data stays on your device at all times
  • We cannot access your data
  • If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device
  • Data is not synchronized to iCloud or any cloud service unless you explicitly enable iOS app backups

Third-Party Services

BikeAnalytics does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No payment processors (the app is free)

Permissions

The app may request the following iOS permissions only if you choose to use specific features:

  • HealthKit/Health App: Only if you choose to import workout data. All data remains on your device.
  • Notifications: Only if you enable workout reminders. No data is sent outside your device.

All permissions are optional and you can revoke them at any time through iOS Settings.

Children's Privacy

Because we don't collect any data, BikeAnalytics is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. However, we recommend parental guidance for all fitness activities.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Important: We will never change our core commitment to not collecting your data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the app, please contact us through our contact page.

Summary

BikeAnalytics is a privacy-first app. We believe your data belongs to you and should stay with you.

  • ❌ No data collection
  • ❌ No tracking or analytics
  • ❌ No third-party services
  • ✅ 100% local processing
  • ✅ Complete control over your data
